Getting Around Tagaytay

Wander more, worry less

The typical bus fare from Manila to Tagaytay ranges from P80 to P150. For flexible planning, you may go via direct or semi-direct routes.

Best Indirect Bus Routes

When you get off onto Tagaytay, the most common drop off place is Tagaytay Rotonda or Olivarez Plaza. A walk around these places can lead you to local eateries, souvenir shops, and the city’s most visited attractions.

Another popular drop off place is Tagaytay-Nasugbu Highway, where you can see the best views of Taal Lake and the volcano. This stretch is also where you’ll find Sky Ranch and several restaurants with postcard-worthy panoramas.

Meanwhile, south buses often pass the Tagaytay-Calamba Road, which is perfect if you’re planning to visit the Tagaytay Picnic Grove, People’s Park in the Sky, or other upland attractions.
