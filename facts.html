Population & Ethnicity

Population (2020): 85,330

Estimated (2024): ~87,800

Ethnic Breakdown:

  • Filipino: ~98%
  • Indigenous Peoples: ~2%

*Exact Tagaytay ethnicity data is limited; values follow Cavite & Region IV-A trends.

Some Indigenous Groups in the Region

Aeta

Skilled hunter-gatherers known for forest knowledge, herbal medicine, and resilience.

Ati

Negrito group with strong traditions in music, dance, and beadwork.

Tagbanua

Among the oldest indigenous groups; noted for rituals, woodwork, and ancient writing.
